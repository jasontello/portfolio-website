Style Guide

A small reusable system for the portfolio: tokens, components, layout rules, and interaction patterns.

v1

Why it exists

I organized the portfolio into a lightweight interface system so the same visual decisions can repeat with more intention.

The goal is not to turn the site into a large product library. It is to document the reusable parts that already shape the portfolio: quiet navigation, restrained cards, direct labels, modal storytelling, and small motion details.
